about

Ailury is a bot currently in development. We aim to make its responses as natural and human-like as possible, while ensuring it is clear that Ailury is a bot and not a human being.

Problems and Solutions

dont you know...

When using language models for learning, you might encounter incorrect answers?! Well we carefully research every response to ensure accuracy, for more productive learning. but please keep in mind that Ailury can still make mistakes, but if she makes a mistake you will know.

dont you know...

If you’re using a language model, you might find that it sometimes provides an answer even when it doesn’t know the correct one. With Ailury, if she doesn’t find a fitting answer, she will let you know that she doesn’t know the answer.

want to play some games?…

For those who enjoy playing games, we have added a variety of fun games to Ailury, such as "Find the Different Emoji" and quizzes. We constantly add new games to the model to keep things exciting.
